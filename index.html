What is Urs forever?

Urs forever is a website made to both inform you and entertain you with information about your favourite artists! This website allows you to find the most authentic information about the artists you love the most, allowing you to learn about their childhood, career and much more!! Urs forever has both free usage options and a premium version, with the premium version having any advantages. The free version of Urs Forever allows customers to find out general information (e.g. real name, genres, awards) about the artists and view some of their best images taken by fans. Along with this Urs Forever lite (free version) also allows users to have a table of some of their artists most famous songs from different albums. Finally each artist also has their own personalised question that they answered in interviews with us which is available under the image gallery. Urs Forever premium allows users to have a better look at their artists with three personalised questions being asked and answered instead of the one. Premium users are also able to add more than 5 artists (unlimited) to their subscriptions unlike lite users. Finally, premium users are able to contact one of their chosen artists on a half-yearly basis and are able to talk to them for 5 minutes. Both premium and lite users are given notifications when new music and concerts hosted by their subscribed artists occur. Overall, Urs forever is everyone's go to music website, personalising music just for you!
